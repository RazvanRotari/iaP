Summary

The aim of the project is to create a platform able to model, query, visualize and recommend media resources (newspapers, podcasts, streams presentations, images, etc.) by making assessments about the quality, reliability and trustworthiness of the provenance meta-data. Metadata is used to represent properties of objects. Many of those properties have to do with provenance, so the two are often equated. Provenance refers to records that describe entities and processes involved in producing and delivering or otherwise influencing that resource. This provenance information is crucial to making assertions about whether information is trusted and how to give credit to originators when reusing information. Provenance assertions are a form of contextual metadata and can themselves become important records with their own provenance.

Introduction

Compared to traditional ways of accessing data of interest, such as RSS aggregators, that provide to the user all the content of the sources they have subscribed to our application aims to offer a more powerful way to review content of interest:

  • podcasts or fresh editorials concerning a specific topic (with specific categories)
  • querying articles based on their meta data and provenance meta-data:
  • articles that contain images
  • articles written in English or Romanian
  • images posted by Romanian photographers or taken in Romania

Recommander system

For the recommender system, the score for a document is calculated based on:

  • the published date of the document;
  • cardinal of the intersection of the document’s categories and the user’s query;
  • the author’s relevance\trustworthiness (provenance metadata).

Instructions

Our application can be used through a web interface or using our API.

In order to use the application using UI you will need to register into our database using the register form. You will be also asked about a couple of categories of articles you would like to see. After registration you will be able to login using credentials that matches the one in the database. Then you will be able to make queries about specific articles and using different kinds of filters.

If you want to use our application as a service for a bigger software or maybe you just want to change the UI, you can use our application through the external api provided.
